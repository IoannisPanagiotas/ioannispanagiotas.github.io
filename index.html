Ioannis Panagiotas

About

I am an INRIA-funded PhD Student in the ROMA team of the LIP laboratory at the ENS de Lyon. My supervisor is Bora Uçar.

My research is in the field of combinatorial scientific computing, focusing primarely on algorithms for matching related problems.
